Dear Volunteer,
 
As the editor of www.iamin.in, I need you to help me with a blog concerning the issue of Road/Foothpath Encroachment, i.e., any form of intrusion or trespassing on the road/footpath. This blog will be published on www.iamin.in.
 
What I need you all to do:
 
  1. Click photos of different instances of road/footpath encroachment. Preferably, do this in and around your locality.
  2. Add a short description with each photo mentioning the area you have clicked the picture in and a brief background on the problem.
 
Criteria:
 
Pictures clicked on cellphone cameras are most welcome. Please do not send in shaky pictures. Pictures from all cities are welcome. You will be given credit for the photos that are published on the I am In website. The last date for sending in pictures is November 17, 2013.
 
Most common examples of footpath encroachment include, but are not limited to:
 
  1. Personal items strewn by shop owners on the sidewalk.
  2. Never Ending Construction/Repairs (this should encroach upon your civic rights; pictures of construction in your housing society are allowed only if they affect people on the streets as well).
  3. Bus stops that were single, standalone poles till recently have now become like flats and a common problem.
  4. Hawkers
  5. Signboards that block footpaths.
 
Looking forward to your entries eagerly.
Thank You!
